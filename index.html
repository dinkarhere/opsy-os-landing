01 Invoicing

Not accounting software. An invoicing app you'll want to open.

Live-preview editor — form on the left, brand-styled invoice on the right, updating as you type. Gapless per-year numbering assigned atomically at finalize. Draft → Sent → Partial → Paid lifecycle. Receipts track partial payments, so the balance is always computed, never wrongly stored.

02 Payments

₹ and UPI, first-class.

The client portal shows a UPI QR code, your UPI ID, and bank details. No gateway, no cut — clients pay you directly and you log the receipt. One share link per client. No accounts to create, no passwords to reset.

Positioning

And a few things Opsy OS isn't.

01

Not accounting software.

No ledgers, journals, GST returns, or TDS. If you need those, keep your accountant.

02

Not a payment processor.

You keep 100%. Clients pay you directly over UPI or bank; you log the receipt.

03

Not a CRM.

A light client model — leads, negotiations, active, closed — not a sales pipeline with stages and forecasts.

04

Not a team tool.

Single-owner workspaces today. If teams come, they'll come deliberately, without gluing on seat pricing.
